a big can of worms

how is this different than blocks?

Blocking, muting, ignoring, or any other of these sorts of actions would, using the scenario above, be on Instance B. This focuses on a different problem (a user‚Äôs digital rights) and attempts to expand it to peer instances, so this would bring User B‚Äôs digital right to Instance A as well.
