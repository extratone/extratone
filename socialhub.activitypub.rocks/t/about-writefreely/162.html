A simple, federated writing platform.

WriteFreely is free and open source software for starting a minimalist, federated blog — or an entire community.

Support the Project

We fund WriteFreely development by offering hosted services. This happens through our flagship instance, Write.as. Buying a paid Write.as account or any of our add-ons directly pays the people building WriteFreely.
