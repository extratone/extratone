The YunoHost community packagers understand that a big part of making federation work is making it possible for more people to get involved in self-hosting their own instances. And that’s why they’re busy with making a lot of the federated applications you already know and use available for self-hosting on YunoHost!

Here’s the list of community applications:

  • https://github.com/YunoHost-Apps/mastodon_ynh
  • https://github.com/YunoHost-Apps/peertube_ynh
  • https://github.com/YunoHost-Apps/reel2bits_ynh
  • https://github.com/YunoHost-Apps/pleroma_ynh
  • https://github.com/YunoHost-Apps/funkwhale_ynh
  • https://github.com/YunoHost-Apps/pixelfed_ynh
  • https://github.com/YunoHost-Apps/writefreely_ynh
  • https://github.com/YunoHost-Apps/anfora_ynh
  • https://github.com/YunoHost-Apps/plume_ynh
  • https://github.com/YunoHost-Apps/friendica_ynh

Each application is packaged by a member of the YunoHost community. To get started, head over to https://yunohost.org/#/ . If you have questions, need support or want to join the YunoHost community, please head over to https://forum.yunohost.org/.
