With hypercapitalist science getting more corrupted all the time there‚Äôs a lot of interest in Open Science, and many proponents are fellow fedizen.

No idea how one would define hypercapitalism, but science is not capitalist. There are no customers, no profits. Politicians and managers like to talk about competition in science, but as there are no customers, this is mostly about abusing the workers more. Don‚Äôt be fooled by their dumb neoliberal rhetoric. That science still works is not because of this fake competition blah blah, but despite it. Because scientists care about science.

Scientific publishing, a parasitic sector, may be called hypercapitalistic, but capitalistic and monopolistic will do.
