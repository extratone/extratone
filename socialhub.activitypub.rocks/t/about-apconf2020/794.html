ActivityPub Conference 2020 Virtual

A conference about the present and future of ActivityPub, the world’s leading federated social web standard. Presentations of prepared talks followed by a loosely structured unconference. #apconf

This category is to prepare, record, and archive the #APConf2020 virtual conference which happens over the Internet from October 2nd to 5th, 2020.

Please see the list of topics at #meeting:apconf2020.

Bandwidth limits:
Please note that the synchronous parts of the conference (including the video calls for the BoFs) will be available to registered participants only.
