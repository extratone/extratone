Hey nice, so just as a recap:

  • You can POST to someone‚Äôs inbox to send them a message (server-to-server / federation only‚Ä¶ this is federation!)
  • You can GET from your inbox to read your latest messages (client-to-server; this is like reading your social network stream)
  • You can POST to your outbox to send messages to the world (client-to-server)
  • You can GET from someone‚Äôs outbox to see what messages they‚Äôve posted (or at least the ones you‚Äôre authorized to see). (client-to-server and/or server-to-server)

Of course, if that last one (GET‚Äôing from someone‚Äôs outbox) was the only way to see what people have sent, this wouldn‚Äôt be a very efficient federation protocol! Indeed, federation happens usually by servers posting messages sent by actors to actors on other servers‚Äô inboxes.
