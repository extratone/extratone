From the beginning, we adopted http://conf.tube to disseminate videos related to the ActivityPub community. The APconf account was created for the #meeting:apconf2020 and remains there to give witness to the work accomplished.

Yesterday I obtained from @rgggn, the admin of conf.tube a dedicated socialhub account for the ActivityPub community at large. I would like here to propose how we could use this account collectively, notably to avoid further hiccups with timely access to the publish button.

What is the socialhub account for?

This section is to define what goes to the video channels

Description: The ActivityPub Community - ConfTube

The SocialHub brings together members of the W3 Social CG, ActivityPub implementors, users, researchers, and other interested people to further the development of the specifications and explore the Fediverse together.

This account provides channels to share ongoing work with the community. For the ActivityPub Conference, see the APConf account.
