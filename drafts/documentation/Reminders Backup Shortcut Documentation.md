## Description

Uses the `Find Reminders` action without filters to retrieve *all* items within the Reminders app in .ics format, shows the result in `Quick Look`, creates a .zip archive of the results and saves it to a location of your choosing as `RemindersBackup[Current Date in ISO 8601].zip`.
